🌿 Welcome To RootNet

Empowering Forest Conservation Through Technology

RootNet is an advanced digital ecosystem developed for the Sri Lankan Forest Department to modernize forest conservation through smart monitoring, biodiversity tracking, wildlife protection, and environmental intelligence.

Built with cutting-edge sensors, satellite imagery, and AI-powered analysis, RootNet helps rangers, scientists, and policymakers make better decisions to protect Sri Lanka's irreplaceable natural heritage.

⚡ What We Do

Core Features of RootNet

Everything you need to monitor, protect, and manage Sri Lanka's forests in one intelligent platform.

🛰️
Smart Monitoring
Real-time satellite and sensor-based monitoring of forest health, coverage changes, and environmental conditions across all protected zones.
🔥
Fire Detection
AI-powered fire detection system that alerts rangers within minutes of smoke or heat anomalies — stopping wildfires before they spread.
🐘
Wildlife Tracking
GPS collar data, camera traps, and AI image recognition to track elephants, leopards, and other endangered wildlife in real time.
🌧️
Climate Intelligence
Weather station integration, rainfall tracking, and micro-climate analysis helping conservationists understand climate change impacts.
🗺️
Forest Mapping
Interactive GIS maps showing forest boundaries, biodiversity hotspots, road networks, and ranger patrol routes updated daily.
📊
Data Analytics
Comprehensive dashboards, trend reports, and exportable datasets for researchers, government bodies, and international conservation partners.

Wildlife We Protect

Vulnerable

Stable
