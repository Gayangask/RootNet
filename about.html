About RootNet

Protecting Forests With Smart Digital Intelligence

RootNet brings conservation, monitoring, emergency response, and biodiversity insights into one connected platform for Sri Lanka's forest future.

Our Purpose

Technology that helps nature stay protected.

RootNet was designed to support forest officers, conservation teams, and environmental decision makers with clearer information about forest health, wildlife activity, threats, and protected ecosystem areas.

By combining real-time monitoring, data-driven reports, and field-ready digital tools, RootNet helps teams respond faster, plan better, and protect biodiversity with confidence.

What We Focus On

Our Vision

Connected conservation for every protected forest.

RootNet's mission is to make forest management more transparent, responsive, and sustainable by giving conservation teams the right information at the right moment.
